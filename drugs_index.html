DRUGS'n'brain

I made these drawings for a project about how our thinking constructs our reality and existence.
Drugs can have an impact on that. The "cigarette kicks" it gives the brain a little impulse to think out of the dull everyday fog. The "alcoho(h)l" (hohl ≙ empty/dumb) reduces the thinking immensely. "Weed distorts" the view into different perspectives. ○○○
