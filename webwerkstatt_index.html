Workspaces

I programmed a website to show the "workspaces" of my fellow students during our online studies.
We spent the whole first semester together, but the only room we shared was a virtual one. While being on one screen with everyone else, each of us was always in an own physical workspace. By clicking on the planets one could find out how these looked like. Watch a video of it here (:
