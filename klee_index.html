About modern art

I did a redesign of the book "about modern art" by Paul Klee from 1945.
It is a theorethical text about visual themes like color, contrast and form. To make the content more accessible, I drew infantile illustrations. I also recreated the font of the old book and did a new layout. Then I printed and bound it. See some snippets (: ○○○○○
